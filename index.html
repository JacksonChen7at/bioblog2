Introduction

Plants are one of the most important living things on Earth. They provide oxygen, food, shelter, and materials that support almost every ecosystem. Without plants, life would not exist in its current form.

Gymnosperms

Gymnosperms are plants that produce “naked seeds,” meaning their seeds are not enclosed in fruits. Conifers like pine trees are common examples. They are adapted to survive in cold and dry environments.

Angiosperms

Angiosperms are flowering plants and the most diverse group in the plant kingdom. They produce seeds inside fruits, which helps with seed protection and spreading.
