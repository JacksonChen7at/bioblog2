Introduction

Plants are essential living organisms that support nearly all life on Earth.

Fun Fact: Plants were the first organisms on land.

Importance of Plants

Plants produce oxygen and form the base of food chains.

Fun Fact: One tree can support multiple humans with oxygen.

Gymnosperms

Seed plants without flowers or fruits.

Fun Fact: Some live over 1000 years.

Angiosperms

Flowering plants and most diverse group.

Fun Fact: They make up most plant life on Earth.
