Introduction

Plants are essential living organisms that support nearly all life on Earth.

Fun Fact: Plants were the first living organisms to colonize land over 400 million years ago.

Importance of Plants

Plants maintain balance in nature and produce oxygen through photosynthesis.

Fun Fact: One large tree can provide enough oxygen for 2–4 people every day.

Gymnosperms

Gymnosperms are seed-producing plants without flowers or fruits.

Fun Fact: Some gymnosperms can live for over 1,000 years.

Angiosperms

Angiosperms are flowering plants and the most diverse group.

Fun Fact: Angiosperms make up about 80% of all plants on Earth.
